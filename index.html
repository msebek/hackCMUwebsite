What is HackCMU?

HackCMU is Carnegie Mellon University's Fall Hackathon. Bring people together for 24 hours and transform a crazy idea into something real. Show off what you've created to your peers, judges, and Corporate sponsors. We've got the food, prizes, and the hack space.
